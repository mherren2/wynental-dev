Il Granito is a renowned natural stone specialist, based in Genk (Belgium) and carved out from a deep-rooted passion for the ancient craft. 

 

Founded in 1992 by Barbara and Vito Rizzi and managed today by Angelo Rizzi, the roots of the thorough craftsmanship reach far back into the family history, deep into the heel of Italy. 

 

Il Granito is active worldwide and a leader in the design and architectural use of natural stone. From New York or Paris to Moscow, with unwavering care and dedication, it aims to excel in all facets of the métier. Angelo Rizzi and his team take nature’s measure for object and project – with an eye for detail – until the solid beauty of natural stone reaches a state of perfection.
